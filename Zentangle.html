In Colombia we continue in difficult times of pandemic. August 2020. In this period and fortunately I have begun to study and through Instagram publications I came to know this method that has accompanied me in long hours of solitude.
The Zentangle Method is an easy-to-learn, relaxing, and fun way to create beautiful images by drawing structured patterns.
